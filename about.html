Mission

Machine learning theory is hard, but it doesn't have to be.

Our goal is to create a platform that can help anyone learn the basics of modern machine learning theory without a background in advanced mathematics or computer science.

We believe that by providing visualizations and removing unnecessary mathematical jargon, we can make machine learning theory accessible to everyone.

Top Priorities

Our top priority is to create great content, share it, and iterate according to the feedback we receieve as quickly as possible.

With this foundation, we hope to improve our web platform and provide features such as a forum, GPU enabled coding environment, and an expert helpline.

Our Dream

Our dream is to create the platform for ML beginners

We want to have the best content, the best community, and the best tools available for anyone willing to learn

We know we have a lot of work to do, but we're excited to get started.
